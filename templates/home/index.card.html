COVID-Classifier

X-ray lung infection detection

This utility will help doctors/radiologist detection of lung infection for multiple frontal chest X-RAYS as we implement the
trained model for more than one image file.
This module will classify the patient's x-ray and give prediction if its infected or not i.e.[NORMAL|COVID-19|PNEUMONIA].
This module will serve in detection of ling infection ans will be beneficial to X-RAY radiologist as their time on observing lung patients X-RAY will get saved.This module is based on deep learning.

Realtime social distancing

Our manuscript focuses on detecting if the people around are maintaining social distancing or not.Using our own self developed for detecting the frame of a person and displaying labels, they are marked as safe or unsafe if the distance is less than a certain value.
From this module Social Distancing violations will get detected and warning highlight will be shown, along with the person who is violating the social distancing Rule.​​

Facemask Detection

In this module, we will be developing a face mask detector which can prove to be beneficial in distinguishing between faces with masks and faces with no masks. ​
This Module is able to detect if the person is wearing face mask or not, it will be good for the surveillance were wearing facemask is mandatory specially in this COVID pandemic situation wearing face mask is Necessary so the surveillance manager can warn those who aren’t wearing facemask without need of observing everyone face.​
